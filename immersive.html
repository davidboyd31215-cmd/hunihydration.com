Step 1

THE SODIUM-POTASSIUM PUMP

3 sodium ions out, 2 potassium ions in. This molecular pump runs 24/7, maintaining the electrical gradient that makes every heartbeat and every step possible. It consumes ~25% of your body's ATP.

Step 2

MUSCLE CONTRACTION

Calcium floods the muscle fiber, triggering actin and myosin to slide past each other. Magnesium then helps the muscle relax. Without this balance, cramps and fatigue take over.
